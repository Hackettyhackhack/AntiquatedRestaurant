Chef Arnaud Michant

As a lover of cuisine and as a chef, Arnaud is passionate about the products he uses.

The Antiquated restaurant is located in Switzerland and makes full use of all of the local access
From fine wines, to cheeses and of course the unique farmers!
Arnaud has worked with some of the best chefs in the world and has decided recently to open a new one called Antiquated.

The whole team at Antiquated believe in mixing ancestral methods with the latest technologies,
Which is why here at Antiquated, we strive for excellence in what is known while also seeking to pioneer through our culinary lab.
